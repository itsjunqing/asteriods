Asteroids Gameplay Introduction

Rocket is given 5 lives. Upon firing each asteroids, player will be awarded with scores,
where the scores are based on the destroy of large or small asteroids.
Destroying larger asteroids will grant the player higher score and smaller asteroids for lower score.
Higher scores will ultimately lead to higher level.
At each increment of level, asteroids will move at a faster rate, so beware of your keys!
When level 3 is reached, a boss will be spawned from the top, this boss will follow the rocket at all times.
If the rocket collides with the boss or the asteroids, the number of lives will be reduced, and the gameplay will be reset.

>> Killing large asteroids with bullets will spawn smaller asteroids and only smaller asteroids can be destroyed fully.

>> To detonate a bomb, press the 'B' button and the bomb will be spawned on the location of the rocket.
This bomb will exist for 10 seconds before it completely disappears into the air.
The bomb will only explode when the enemy boss is nearby, and if it is, the enemy's health will be reduced by 1.
When the health reaches zero, the enemy is destroyed

Leveling System

Each level will be increased when the score reaches above 1000 * current level.
For instance, when at level 1, level 2 will be achieved when score is 1000 (1000 * 1).
At level 2, level 3 will be achieved when score is 2000 (1000 * 2).
This leveling system applies for all levels.

How To Play

  1. ArrowUp Key : To move/thrust the rocket forward (BEWARE OF SPEEDING, you may collide with asteroid! use ArrowDown to stop moving!)
  2. ArrowDown Key : To stop moving/thrusting the rocket forward (to reduce speed / stop moving)
  3. ArrowLeft Key : To rotate to the left
  4. ArrowRight Key : To rotate to the right
  5. Space Key : To fire bullets
  6. B Key : To detonate bomb (only for killing enemy boss)

How To Kill

  • For asteroids: Kill them with bullets by pressing 'Space' key
  • For enemy boss: Kill them with bombs by pressing 'B' key, which will reduce the enemy's health
    (Hint: the goal is to move around the map, spawn bomb and nudge them to move over the bomb, because the boss will follow you!)
